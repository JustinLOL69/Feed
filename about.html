About Fabric-en-3D

Fabric-en-3D is a modern 3D printing service dedicated to transforming ideas into real, functional objects. Founded by Jamael Mackoosing, the business focuses on delivering high-quality, affordable, and customizable 3D printed products in Mauritius.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make 3D printing accessible to everyone by providing innovative solutions for everyday problems, prototypes, and creative projects.

Why Choose Us

  • ✔ High-quality PLA printing
  • ✔ Custom designs tailored to your needs
  • ✔ Affordable pricing
  • ✔ Fast and reliable service

Meet the Founder

Jamael Mackoosing is passionate about technology, design, and entrepreneurship. With experience in 3D printing and product creation, he aims to build a service that helps individuals and businesses bring their ideas to life.
